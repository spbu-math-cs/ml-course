. $x$ , $\mu_i, i = 1, \dots, m, \lambda_j, j = 1, \dots, k$:

$ \begin{cases} \frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial x} = 0,\ \mathcal{L}(x; \mu, \lambda) = f(x) + \sum\limits_{i=1}^m \mu_i g_i(x) + \sum\limits_{j=1}^k \lambda_j h_j(x) \\ g_i(x) \leq 0, h_j(x) = 0\ (\text{ }) \\ \mu_i \geq 0\ (\text{ }) \\ \mu_i g_i(x) = 0\ (\text{ }) \end{cases} $
